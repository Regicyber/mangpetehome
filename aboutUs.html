WHO WE ARE

Our business is the production and distribution of high quality, organic meals at affordable prices.
Our farms are designed with nature in mind to minimize our carbon footprints and the use of harmful substances like pesticides and inorganic fertilizer.

Our primary focus is on growing the crops and livestock necessary for a balanced diet in children and adults.
We achieve this by using agricultural best practices to build upon modern farming techniques like Hydroponics and Aquaponics to produce organic crops quicker than conventional farming.

Our mission is to make hunger a thing of the past by creating a robust network of healthy and sustainable farms and making the produce available in easy to cook, organic containers.

How we work

M&P is made up of a bunch of people who care about healthy meals and sustainable farming practices. 
We have taken it upon ourselves to preach the to the world that it is possible to farm without the need for harmful chemicals.

Together with our team of agri-business experts, we supervise and manage the production, distribution and storage of farm produce the world over.

We are on the edge of the agricultural revolution by integrating novel farming technologies like IOT monitoring systems, Aquaponics and Hydroponics systems. These are used in combination with our already robust network of greenhouse management and installation specialists.
